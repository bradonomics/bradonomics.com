Linux Note Taking (Alternatives to Evernote)

Note: this document is a draft and may make no sense.

I started working on this September 14, 2021.

The last time I updated it was September 14, 2021.

Simplenote

https://simplenote.com/

Benefits

  • Built by Automattic.
  • Inner linking of notes.

Drawbacks

  • Built by Automattic.
  • No notebook system, only tags.

Joplin

https://joplinapp.org/

Benefits

  • Notebooks and tags
  • Sync your note files to your provider of choice. No vender lock-in.
  • Web clipper (Chrome & FireFox).

Drawbacks

  • No inner linking.
