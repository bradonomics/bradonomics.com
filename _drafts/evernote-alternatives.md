## Simplenote

<https://simplenote.com/>

**Benefits**

- Built by Automattic.
- Inner linking of notes.

**Drawbacks**

- Built by Automattic.
- No notebook system, only tags.

## Joplin

<https://joplinapp.org/>

**Benefits**

- Notebooks and tags
- Sync your note files to your provider of choice. No vender lock-in.
- Web clipper (Chrome & FireFox).

**Drawbacks**

- No inner linking.
